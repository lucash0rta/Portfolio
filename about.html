About Me & CV

Lucas Horta

3D Artist / Developer

Hi, my name is Lucas Horta. I'm passionate about my creative practice; I spend most of my time reading, lurking in discords, making creative code projects, Zbrush sculpting or going on content deep dives about my latest obsessions.

My practice is technically focused, and with each project, I learn new skills. From complex rigging and interaction design using C# and the OpenXR input framework to optimizing WebGL Unity builds for digital gallery spaces, I'm constantly expanding my capabilities.

I hope to continue expanding my practice and pushing the boundaries of what's possible in 3D art and development.
