Phoria

  • Location: Melbourne, Australia
  • Specialization: VR and AR content
  • Notable Projects: Collaborations with Netflix and David Attenborough
  • Potential Roles: VR Developer, 3D Artist, Creative Technologist
  • Average Salary: 68k to 72k AUD (according to GlassDoor.com.au)
  • Personal Experience: Attended showcase at Melbourne University

Liminal VR

  • Location: Melbourne, Australia
  • Specialization: VR application development
  • Notable Projects: Liminal application on Meta Quest headset (2019)
  • Work Environment: Currently remote-friendly, interns working completely remotely
  • Personal Experience: Used apps in 2019, not very impressed with their work
  • Potential: Short-term experience, prefer more engaged companies

Studio Brave Melbourne

  • Location: Melbourne, Australia
  • Specialization: Diverse portfolio from boutique brand identities to large-scale campaigns
  • Key Strengths: Strong sense of storytelling and attention to detail
  • Relevant Skills: Motion graphics prevalent in their works
  • Personal Note: Potential to leverage personal skills in motion graphics and aesthetics

Rising Sun Pictures (RSP)

  • Location: Adelaide, South Australia, with a studio in Brisbane
  • Founded: 1995
  • Specialization: High-end visual effects for film and television
  • Notable Projects: The Lord of the Rings, Harry Potter series, Marvel films
  • Estimated Entry-Level Salary: AUD 50,000 - AUD 70,000 per year
  • Training Programs: Offers internships for students and recent graduates
  • Personal Note: Studied VFX/Game design certificate in Adelaide, need to develop 3D skills further

Superbright Studios

  • Location: New York, NY, USA and London, UK
  • Founded: 2013
  • Specialization: Immersive experiences, VR, AR, and interactive installations
  • Notable Projects: More Poltical and art focused installations; ie; partnership between Vortic Art and Frieze
  • Estimated Entry-Level Salary: USD 50,000 - USD 70,000 per year
  • Company Culture: Collaborative, experimental, encouraging cross-disciplinary exploration

ZEITGUISED

  • Location: Berlin, Germany
  • Founded: 2001, based in Berlin since 2009
  • Specialization: Autonomous form studio for audio-visual, experiential, and spatial installations
  • Focus: Innovative brand images, campaigns, and multi-sensory installations
  • Notable Project: "empancipath" for Kvadrat (2017)
  • Style: Seamless blend of physical and digital spaces, organic and geometric forms

empancipath: A project showcasing ZEITGUISED's ability to create seamless transformations between organic and geometric forms. Created for Kvadrat's "My Canvas" exhibition at London Design Festival 2017, it demonstrates the studio's expertise in blending physical and digital spaces.
